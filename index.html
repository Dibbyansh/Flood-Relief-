ABOUT

Catastrophic floods due to incessant rains cause wreaked havoc in low-lying areas and valleys. Even with the precautions taken floods are a great blow to local economy and livelihood. Recent times have seen the ill-effects of flood especially in Assam valley, north Bihar, Kerela and Chennai suburb. In all these cases we have seen infrastructure being destroyed, farmlands being washed-away , people becoming homeless. In these tough times we should all unite to help them rehabilitate to their normal lives.
In rural areas flood can rip of the community of their economy. Water from flood destroys farmlands and granaries. This can vacuum in food supply can increase rates and cause food shortage. Death of an earning member can be the worst case scenario. Lives can stop functioning normally . Your donation can help them come back to their normal lives. It can repair all the damage done and give the ones who suffered an opportunity to excel

A contribution made in kind can help the affected rebuild homes and schools. This can in turn increase literacy. Your little contribution can bring smile to millions, grant them good health and sanitation post flood and make their livelihood sustain. This is our time to contribute as much as possible and be a reason for their well being.
